Cloud Infrastructure & Kubernetes Consulting for Teams That Ship

Breakfast Technologies partners with startups and scale-ups to design, build, and operate infrastructure that scales with your ambitions. We engineer for high availability and low latency from day one, because your users expect fast, and your business can't afford down. From first deployment to full production scale, we handle the infrastructure so your team can stay focused on the product.

Cloud Infrastructure

Technologies We Work With

AWS Solutions Architecture

We bring certified solutions architect expertise to every engagement, designing scalable, production-ready cloud architecture across the full AWS ecosystem — from CloudFront and Route53 to API Gateway and beyond. Grounded in the AWS Well-Architected Framework, every system we deliver is built for security, reliability, and operational excellence from day one, with architecture decisions that won't need rethinking tomorrow.

Cloud Migrations

End-to-end cloud migrations built around your actual workload, whether that's lift-and-shift, re-platforming, or a full re-architecture. We assess your existing environment, design the target state, and manage the cutover to minimise downtime. Resource sizing is right-sized from the start, keeping costs lean without sacrificing reliability or performance under load.

Infrastructure as Code

Terraform and Ansible-driven environments that are reproducible, version-controlled, and consistent across development, staging, and production. We build modular Terraform configurations, including complex reusable modules, with remote state managed via S3 and DynamoDB for locking and consistency. Beyond core resources, we extend IaC coverage to Helm chart deployments, Docker provisioning, and shell-based automation, keeping your entire stack under version control.

Security Hardening

Security embedded at every layer of the stack, not bolted on at the end. We enforce least-privilege IAM policies, harden network boundaries with security groups and NACLs, and encrypt data at rest and in transit. Vulnerability scanning and compliance checks are built into the pipeline so your infrastructure is hardened before it reaches production, and stays that way.

High Availability

Infrastructure designed for high availability from day one, with multi-AZ deployments, load balancing, and automated failover built in from the start. We target defined uptime SLAs, design for graceful degradation under failure conditions, and use health checks and circuit breakers to prevent cascading outages, treating downtime and latency spikes as design failures, not operational surprises.

Monitoring & Observability

Cloud-native monitoring stacks with real-time visibility into workload health and system performance. We instrument for the metrics that matter — CPU, memory, request rates, latency percentiles, error rates, and disk I/O — alongside scaling signals like queue depth and custom CloudWatch metrics that feed directly into autoscaling decisions. Alerting pipelines and distributed tracing ensure you're never operating blind.

Kubernetes Management

Technologies We Work With

Managed Kubernetes Services

Full cluster lifecycle management on EKS, GKE, and AKS, from initial provisioning through to day-two operations. We own the platform so your engineers can own the product.

Zero-Trust Cluster Security

Modern Kubernetes security goes well beyond basic access control. Credentials are short-lived, workloads are isolated, and least-privilege is enforced at runtime. Tools like OIDC-backed IRSA, pod and container security contexts, and granular RBAC policies are how we get there, but the approach is always shaped around your threat model and compliance requirements.

Service Mesh & Ingress Architecture

Kubernetes networking at production scale demands more than default configurations. We design the right stack for your topology, whether that's Cilium with BGP peering or overlay routing, NGINX Ingress for external traffic, or HashiCorp's service mesh for secure inter-service communication.

Autoscaling & Resilience

Your platform should scale to demand without anyone touching it. We use tools like HPA, AWS Cluster Autoscaler, and proactive pre-warming strategies to eliminate cold-start latency before it reaches your users, with the right combination depending on your traffic profile.

GitOps & CI/CD

Automated, auditable deployments from commit to production with no manual handoffs and no environment drift. We typically use GitHub Actions and AWS CodeBuild with Helm managing releases and rollbacks, adapted to whatever stack your team is already on.

Unified Observability

Full visibility into cluster health, workload performance, and application behaviour, surfaced in a single coherent stack. Prometheus, Grafana, Datadog, FluentBit, and CloudWatch are the tools we reach for, composed into a setup that fits your infrastructure.
